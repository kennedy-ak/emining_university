Welcome to E-miningCampus

Welcome to E-miningCampus, your premier online learning platform for mining engineering education and training. We are a team of experienced lecturers from the Department of Mining Engineering, University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa, Ghana, with a passion for sharing our knowledge and expertise with the global mining community.

Established in June 2024, E-miningCampus is a response to the growing demand for flexible and accessible mining education and training. Our platform offers a range of online courses, expresso certificates, proficiency certificates and professional certificate programs in mining engineering, designed to equip learners with the skills and competencies required to succeed in the industry.
